Heart Failure Prediction

This GitHub repository is a Data Science Project attempting to predict Heart Failure among patients. Heart failure is a progressive condition in which the heart is unable to pump enough blood to meet the body’s oxygen demand. Cardiovascular disease causes 17.9 million deaths per year, making it the leading cause of death globally. Additionally, heart disease cost the United States $363 billion in 2016 due to health care services, medicine, and lost productivity.
